Overview

Places Covered : Ramoji Film City, Snow World, Birla mandir, Salarjung Museum, Charminar, Mecca Masjid, Golkonda Fort etc

Itinerary

  • 1

    Day

    Cochin to Hyderabad Flight Arrive Hyderabad-Lumbini Park-Laser Show-Hussain Sagar Lake

    The first day of your fantastic Hyderabad tour will begin with your arrival at Hyderabad Airport. Our representative will receive guests at airport. Guests will be escorted to a hotel booked previously for your comfortable stay in the city.After getting freshed up, tour starts with the briefing about the places to be visited.Lumbini Park is one of the beautiful parks in the twin cities Hyderabad and Secunderabad located along the banks of Hussain Sagar Lake.It has become an ideal place of relaxation and a place of amusement. It is a hit with kids and families and has several attractions that could lure you to spend an evening away from home, with your family. The spectacular musical dancing fountain and water cascade dance to the tunes of popular numbers from Hindi and Telugu film songs (Laser Show). This is one of the must see sight and almost the park is crowded with visitors and picnickers. overnight stay.

  • 2

    Day

    Ramoji Film City and Snow World

    After breakfast Day tour starts to Ramoji film city,World's largest film studio.A fast growing tourist destination. Ramoji Film City is a land of movies, entertainment and magic, it has everything that dreams are made of. Filmcity offers unlimited fun, thrill and excitement and it’s a great visual extravaganza. The place is a desired destination for everyone.By 4 pm in the evening the whole crew will move to next attraction of Hyderabad,ie.,Snow World. Snow Worldis a snow theme,India’s first and World’s best snow theme park helps to experience snow fall and polar climate as if it is a polar region. By using art technology, in this snow park, artificially snow is produced and allowed to fall which almost offers a real look. At the time of entry, to all the customers waterproof shoes, gloves and some warm clothes is provided by the park members so as to protect from the cold climate inside due to continues falling of snow. overnight stay.

  • 3

    Day

    Birla mandir-Salarjung Museum-Charminar-Mecca Masjid-Golkonda Fort

    The Third day of this tour to Hyderabad will be attributed to a full excursion of the city. The tour often starts early in the morning. The sightseeing tour of the city will begin with a morning visit to the Birla Mandir post breakfast.Later Visit Salarjung Museum.Charminar,Mecca Masjid and Golkonda Fort,One of the most magnificent fortress complexes in India .overnight stay.

  • 4

    Day

    Hyderabad local sightseeing continues…

    After breakfast proceed to visit Nehru Zoological Park, Visit NTR Gardens and IMAX theatre. ( or Optional visit to Planetorium and Science museum ).Evening free for shopping and personal activities.Oevrnight stay.

  • 5

    Day

    Hyderabad-Departure

    Morning after breakfast guests will be transferred to the International Airport from where they will board the flight back home. Tour ends with Sweet Memories..
