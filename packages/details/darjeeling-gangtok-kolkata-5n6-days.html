Itinerary

  • 1

    Day

    Cochin-Bagdogra
    Meal Plan: Dinner

    Flight journey from Cochin to Bagdogra. Our representative will receive you at Bagdogra Airport and transfer to Gangtok by road via Pashupathinagar (Indo Nepal boarder), Mirik Lake on and check in to the Hotel booked previously for your comfortable stay in the city. Overnight Stay in Hotel at Darjeeling

  • 2

    Day

    In Darjeeling
    Meal Plan: Breakfast-Lunch-Dinner

    Early morning tour to Tiger Hills (8364 Ft / 2550 Mts at 3.30 AM) to view sunrise over Kanchendzonga Peak (Subject to weather condition). On way back visit Ghoom Monastery, BatasiaLoop and back to hotel . After breakfast go for a 7 point seeing of Japanese Temple, Peace pagoda, Padmajanaidu Himalayan Zoological Park & Himalayan Mountaineering Institute (Closed on Thursday), Tenzing Rock, & outer view of a Tea Estate, Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Centre Back to hotel. Afternoon free time for leisure or personal Tour. Overnight Stay in Hotel at Darjeeling.

  • 3

    Day

    Darjeeling –Gangtok (approx. 90km/04 Hrs)
    Meal Plan: Breakfast-Lunch-Dinner

    After Breakfast Proceed to Gangtok On Arrival Gangtok Local Sightseeing 

    Ganesh Tok- from this spot one gets birds view of Sprawling Gangtok town. 
    Tashi Viewpoint- From this point you get A breathtaking view of Mount Kanchenjunga. 
    Gangtok Ropeway- It is a Kilometer ride from Chodten Gompa Gate to Sikkim press. 
    Flower Show- This is the venue for annual orchid show. The flowers are available to see All year long. 
    Institute of Tibetology- This Buddhist Institute is a premiere Institute and Research Centre. 
    The main attraction here is the museum which houses around 200 Buddhist icons, vast collection of rare 
    Lepcha, Tibetan and Sanskrit manuscripts, statues etc – Overnight Stay in Hotel at Gangtok

  • 4

    Day

    In Gangtok
    Meal Plan: Breakfast-Lunch-Dinner

    After breakfast, proceed for Permit formalities to visit Nattulla Pass, (14,200ft)
    Tsomgo Lake (12,400 ft.) &New Baba Mandir (Subject to Permit Availability) or Return back to Gangtok by afternoon. Evening at leisure. Overnight Stay in Hotel at Gangtok. (In case of Land slide or any other reason Tsomgo Lake is closed No other alternate sightseeing.)

  • 5

    Day

    Gangtok – Bagdogra – Cochin (Approx. 100Km/4Hrs)
    Meal Plan : Breakfast (Subject to flight timings)

    Early morning check out from hotel and transfer to Airport for return flight to Cochin.

Important Facts for Traveller’s:
Nathula Pass: (We can not give you any guarantee for nathulla pass but we will try)

  • Nathula visit is subject to availability of permit & road condition. It can be done on the same day with Tsomgo Lake – Baba Mandir, except Monday and Tuesday. Only limited permits are issued by the Sikkim Tourism Department before 24 hrs. of tour (OR on the day of tour depends on road condition) to those travel agencies registered with Sikkim Tourism Department on Rotation basis. It is closed on Mon & Tue. NAME, ADDRESS, AGE & GENDER i.e. naming list of all guests traveling are required for processing of permit. Normally organizing permit at the eleventh hour is not possible, minimum time required is 72 hours before the arrival to apply the permit with all documents. We will try our best, but in case of non-availability of permit, the tour will only be till Baba Mandir. In case the permit is available one can opt for the trip with an extra charge approx. Rs. 5,500/- per vehicle.
  • It is mandatory that guest should carry photo proof identity card such as Voter Id / Passport / Driving Licence and passport size photograph. (PAN Card & Aadhar Card are not considered as a valid ID proof). For above 05 yrs. child School ID card is valid.
  • As permit is mandatory and very limited number of permit are issued per day for visiting Nathula, Guest have to travel by any car whichever is available (like Xylo/Scorpio- Maxx/Bolero/Sumo) as per permit issued. We cannot give any choice on Vehicle type. This is to inform you that in order to control and send more tourists to Nathula pass, the department of tourism after consulting with travel agents laid down the following rules :-
  • In case of less than 6 people from one travel agent the department generally club small number with another travel agent’s small number of tourists just for 4 km up and down before Nathula pass.
  • The Indian Army allows visiting Nathula from 11:00AM to 12:30PM only .Guest have to start early from Gangtok. If guest makes delay in starting ex Gangtok we cannot guarantee the visit in spite of having confirmed permit.
  • In high season limited permit will be issued by authorities. We will try to get the permit but if we will not get the permit then we will refund the supplement amount after end of the tour.
  • Please note if Nathula pass permit not done due to weather conditions / Land slide / any other reason than we will refund the supplement amount after end of the tour.
  • Also note that if Nathula Pass permit is issued and due to any other reason they can’t visit Nathula Pass then we are unable to refund anything.
