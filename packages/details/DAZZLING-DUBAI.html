Itinerary

  • 1

    Day

    Cochin – Dubai
    Meal Plan : Dinner

    Assemble at Cochin international airport, after emigration and customs formalities flight depart to Dubai.

    Dubai

    Sightseeing:  Dhow Cruise

    Arrive at Dubai International Airport, Welcome to UAE, the land of Sand.  Meet our local representative and proceed to the Dubai. Check into hotel . Later in the evening we welcome you on-board to take pleasure in a Night Cruise along the stunning Dubai Marina.

    The Cruise glides along the Dubai Marina in an elegant and traditional Arabian Dhow, contrasted by the modern, expressive skyscrapers along the Dubai Marina.  During the Dubai Marina Dhow Dinner cruise, be awed by the glittering lights, open seas, and unique architecture complimented by lounge music, and an international buffet dinner. The sparkling skyscrapers under the moonlit sky will wow your senses. An international buffet with something for everyone and soothing trendy music makes this Marina Dhow Cruise night a long lasting remembrance!

  • 2

    Day

    Dubai Sightseeing: Dubai Frame & Global Village
    Meal Plan: Breakfast-Lunch-Dinner

    Morning after breakfast we will visit the Dubai frame  .The Dubai Frame is an architectural landmark in Zabeel Park, Dubai. It holds the record for the largest frame in the world. Whilst described by The Guardian newspaper as "the biggest picture frame on the planet, it is also controversial as the "biggest stolen building of all time. The project was conceived by Fernando Donis, and selected as the winner of a design competition by the Government of Dubai. The designer has alleged that he had his intellectual property stolen and was denied credit for the design. Later Proceed to visit Global Village located in Dubai Land, the worlds largest and the region’s first premier cultural,Entertainment and shopping destination, welcoming more than four million guests per year. Dinner at Indian Restaurant.

  • 3

    Day

    Dubai Sightseeing: Miracle Garden & Desert Safari
    Meal Plan : Breakfast-Lunch-Dinner.

    Morning after breakfast proceed to visit Miracle Garden, the most beautiful and biggest natural flower garden and the longest flower wall in the world. More than 45 million flowers on display, the Dubai Miracle garden has truly earned its place as the world’s largest garden. The Dubai Miracle Garden is unique in that it was essentially built on a desert. Head landscaper Akar says that the grounds are a great example of how it is possible to “green the desert” by reusing waste water. Designers took additional precautions to protect the beautiful garden arrangements from the environment. For instance, the garden’s perimeter is lined with trees that are used as windbreakers. Unlike most of the gardens around the world, the Dubai Miracle Garden closes during the summer due to severe weather condition.

    Evening, we set out for the Desert Wonder- the popular Desert Safari.  This tour departs in the afternoon across the desert of Dubai with several photo-stops. The drive continues across the desert. We stop to watch the beautiful sunset before reaching our campsite where you have the opportunity to do camel riding, sand boarding and try out a henna design on hands or feet. Enjoy Tea, cool drinks etc. After working up an appetite enjoy a delicious barbecue dinner and a shisha (the famous Arabic water pipe). Before returning to Dubai watch the belly dance performance and the campfire by starlight.

  • 4

    Day

    Sightseen : City Tour & Burj Khalifa
    Meal Plan: Breakfast - Lunch – Dinner

    After breakfast, proceed for Dubai city tour.  This Tour offers a wide horizon of some of the city’s most beautiful landmarks. We drive through the Dubai Creek; we stop at the magnificent Jumeirah Mosque, which is a fine example of modern Islamic architecture built in 1979. The mosque is one of the most frequently visited and photographed sight. We then stop at the Jumeirah Public Beach, pass by Jumeirah Beach Hotel and stop at Burj Al Arab - the most luxurious and forth tallest hotel in the world. The hotel soars to a height of 321 meters dominating the Dubai coastline. We continue our journey as we go to the Palm Island and at its crescent you see the Atlantis hotel. Lunch at Indian Restaurant. Later proceed to Dubai Mall for a wonderful shopping experience.

    A chance to see underwater world and a memorable experience. Climb up to the top of Burj Khalifa- tallest man made structure in the world and the magnificent centerpiece of Downtown Dubai- a new, world-class destination and when you are on top you get a breathtaking view of Dubai.  It’s amazing!! We can climb up to 124th floor of the tower. Shopping and later at night drop to Airport for return flight .
