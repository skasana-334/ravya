Itinerary

  • 1

    Day

    Kochi to Kathmandu by Flight
    Meal Plan: Dinner

    Evening visit Pashupatinath temple, Situated 5 km east of Katmandu, The temple of Lord Shiva. Pashupathinath with 2-tiered golden roof and silver doors is famous for super architecture.

    Visitors can clearly see the temple and the activities performed in the temple premises from eastern bank of Bagmati River Overnight stay at Kathmandu.

  • 2

    Day

    Kathmandu to Pokhara (Approx 215 Km, 8hrs drive)
    Meal Plan: Breakfast-Lunch-Dinner

    On the way , visit Manakamna Devi Temple [by Cable Car].     This place is famous for the temple of Manakamna, one of the manifestations of the Hindu goddess Bhagwati. Manakamna temple is located 12 Km south of historic town Gorkha and situated on a prominent ridge (1302 mt. above sea level) overlooking the river valleys of Trisuli (south) and Marshyangdi (west). The unique location of this place is dramatized during winter when the ridge appears as an island above the sea of morning mist. People believe that Manakamna Devi fulfills the wishes of her devotees, thus called the wish fulfilling Deity. Manakamana is the first place where Cable car was introduced in Nepal and became extremely popular among the locals as well as the tourists. This cable car is in Kurintar, from where Manakamna is 10 minutes distance Via cable car. Kurintar is around 105 KM west of Kathmandu on the highway to Pokhara Evening arrival at Pokhra. Overnight stay atPokhra.

  • 3

    Day

    Pokhra local Sight seeing
    Meal Plan: Breakfast-Lunch-Dinner.

    After breakfast, start the sightseeing tour at 9:00 AM. Proceed to Sethi River, The Seti Gandaki River, also known as the Seti River or the Seti Khola, is a river of western Nepal, a left tributary of the Trishuli River. Its gorges around Pokhara are a major attraction for tourists worldwide. Fewa Lake, the biggest lake in the Pokhara, Annapurna Temple is a tiered temple dedicated to the main goddess of Ason – Annapurna Ajima. The temple was built under the Shah dynasty, in 1893 and then proceed to Devi’s waterfall, Visitors can try their luck on the luck pond constructed there by throwing and placing the coin on the statue of God. Likewise, one can find a model of traditional typical Nepali house and a series of statue of Nepali people wearing traditional dresses where visitors can click photo. Now get ready to see Gupthashewar cave, cave shrine dedicated to Shiva. Overnight stay at Pokhra

  • 4

    Day

    Pokhra to Kathmandu (Approx 215kms 8hrs drive)
    Meal Plan: Breakfast-Lunch-Dinner

    After breakfast Proceed to Kathmandu .Evening reach at Kathmandu. Overnight stay at Kathmandu

  • 5

    Day

    Kathmandu Local Sight Seeing
    Meal Plan: Breakfast-Lunch-Dinner

    After breakfast, visit Bouddhanath Stupa which lies 8 km east of Kathmandu. The colossal ancient stupa is one of the biggest in the world. After lunch, visit Swayambhunath, one of the world’s most glorious Buddhists chaityas. It is said to be 2000 years old, painted on 4 sides of the spire bases are the all-seeing eyes of Lord Buddha. Then visit Guheswari Temple , a very sacred temple which is dedicated to the Goddess Parvati. According to legend, the wife of Lord Shiva, Parvati sacrificed her life by burning herself to death in her father’s ritual fire. Her distressed husband then chose to wonder the world carrying the body of his dead wife as he did so. No words to explain the site other than posting the photos. Visit Durbar Square and Jalnarayan Temple [sleeping Vishnu] Overnight Stay in Kathmandu hotel.

     

    Note * Early Morning, Mountain flight [optional]

    Mountain flight in Nepal is one of the most exclusive mountain fly tour packages in the world which provides a scenic view of Mount Everest-Top of the World ( 8848.86 M height) flight tour along with other series of Giant panoramic Himalayan ranges, long-ranged Tibetan plateau up to the closer distance 5 nautical miles

  • 6

    Day

    Kathmandu – Kochi
    Meal Plan: Breakfast - Lunch

    Get transferred to Tribhuvan International Airport. Tour ends with sweet memories
