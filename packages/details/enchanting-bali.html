Itinerary

  • 1

    Day

    Cochin – Kuala Lumpur

    Assemble at Cochin International Airport at 21:20 hrs. Departure by Malindo Flight OD 232  at 00:20 hrs to Kuala Lumpur. 

  • 2

    Day

    Bali – Uluwatu Temple
    Meal Plan - Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

    Arrive in Kuala Lumpur at 7.05 hrs and take connection Flight to Bali at 09.10 hrs.  You will arrive in Bali at  12.20hrs. Welcome to Bali! the island of God. After customs and immigration process, you will be greeted by our guide outside the baggage hall area and will be taken to an Indian restaurant for lunch. After lunch, we check-in to the hotel. Later, you will be taken to Uluwatu Temple. Uluwatu Temple is a Balinese Hindu sea temple located in Uluwatu. The temple is regarded as one of the sad kahyangan and is dedicated to Sang Hyang Widhi Wasa in his manifestation as Rudra. The temple  is built at the edge of a 70-meter-high (230 ft) cliff or rock  projecting into the sea.In folklore, this rock is said to be part of Dewi Danu's petrified barque. The temple is inhabited by monkeys, who are notorious for snatching visitors' belongings. Though a small temple was claimed to have existed earlier, the structure was significantly expanded by a Javanese sage, Empu Kuturan in the 11th Century. Another sage from East Java, Dang Hyang Nirartha is credited for constructing the padmasana shrines. In the evening, you will dine at an Indian restaurant and will be driven to the hotel for overnight stay.


    Meal Plan: Lunch and Dinner

  • 3

    Day

    Ubud – Kintamani Tour
    Meal Plan - Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

    After breakfast, the tour will begin with watching Barong Dance, then visit Celuk the center of gold and silver works, Mas the center of woodcarvings, Ubud the center of Balinese paintings. Ubud is a town on the Indonesian island of Bali in Ubud District, located amongst rice paddies and steep ravines in the central foothills of the Gianyar regency. Promoted as an arts and culture centre, it has developed a large tourism industry.Ubud's population of about 74,320 people] is dwarfed by the more than 3 million foreign tourists who visit every year]. The area surrounding the town is made up of small farms, rice paddies, agroforestry plantations, and tourist accommodations. As of 2018, more tourists visited Ubud than Denpasar in southern Bali.Your Lunch at Indian Restaurant. After lunch the tour will move to Kintamani, the highlight of this tour is the magnificent view of mount and Lake Batur with the smoky Agung Volcano in the background. Kintamani is a village on the western edge of the larger caldera wall of Gunung Batur in Bali, Indonesia. It is on the same north-south road as Penelokan and has been used as a stopping place to view the Gunung Batur region. Kintamani is also known for Pura Tuluk Biyu's 1,000-year-old "Rites of Peace" stone tablets and the Kintamani dog. It is situated next to Mount Batur. After that, you will be taken to visit the holy springs of Tirta Empul at Tampak Siring. In the evening, you will be transferred to an Indian restaurant for Dinner and overnight stay at a hotel.

  • 4

    Day

    Mengwi – Bedugul – Kedaton – Tanah Lot Sunset
    Meal Plan - Breakfast,Lunch & Dinner

    After breakfast, you will be transferred to Taman Ayun mengwi royal palace, after which you will be taken to Bedugul to visit the Ulun Danu Temple. Lunch would be provided at Indian restaurant and you will be taken to The Monkey Forest. Later, proceed to visit Tanah Lot Temple. Tanah Lot is a rock formation off the Indonesian island of Bali. The temple is home to a number of pilgrims and a popular tourist and cultural icon for photography
    Tanah Lot is claimed to be the work of the 16th-century Dang Hyang Nirartha. During his travels along the south coast he saw the rock-island's beautiful setting and decided to rest there. Some fishermen saw him, and bought him gifts. Nirartha then spent the night on the little island. Later he spoke to the fishermen and told them to build a shrine on the rock, for he felt it to be a holy place to worship the Balinese sea gods.The main deity of the temple is Dewa Baruna or Bhatara Segara, who is the sea god or sea power and these days, Nirartha is also worshipped here. If the weather permits, you will see the beautiful sunset. After dinner from a restaurant, you will be taken back to hotel for overnight stay.
      
     

  • 5

    Day

    Water sport Activity & Turtle Island Visit– Bounty sunset Dinner Cruise
    Meal Plan - Breakfast,Lunch & Dinner

    After breakfast, you will be transferred to Tanjung Benoa for Watersport activities (Inclusive 01x Banana Boat,Turtle Island visit on glass bottom boat and other activity on personal account).  While you have a turtle island tour, you will be taken to the sea as far as 300 – 500 meters offshore with a glass bottom boat.

    While on a glass bottom boat, you will be accompanied by the boat driver. From the glass bottom boat, you can enjoy the beautiful underwater scenery without having to join the scuba dive. Coral reefs, a variety of ornamental fish, marine plants and other marine animals. Do not forget to bring bread so that the kids can feed the fish while on the boat. This trip lasts about 15 minutes.

    After seeing and feeding the fish, the tour will proceed to turtle island conservation, which will take time about  15 minutes. After arriving on the turtle island of Tanjung Benoa, we will be happy to invite you to see the green turtles. Lunch would be provided at an Indian Restaurant.

    Bounty sunset Dinner Cruise

    Bounty Sunset Dinner Cruise offers an elegance lifestyle to enjoy the tasteful dine on the cruise ship in the romantic ambience. This cruise departs from Benoa Port to surround the coastal area of Benoa and serves the International Buffet Dinner with spectacular dance performance as well as live music. It is of the exciting Bali Sunset Cruises ideally for anyone who loves the exclusive lifestyle and romantic experience during holidays in Bali. The cruise is well equipped with complete facilities with professional and friendly crews to keep your cruise trip comfortable. Also, Bounty Sunset Dinner Cruise will take you on board to enjoy the romantic sunset and dinner within 3 hours. Moreover, you will have the opportunity to enjoy interactive performances. It is ideal for individual or group booking to join the live party on board. Benoa Harbor for Sunset Dinner Cruise. After dinner, overnight stay in hotel.

    Day

    Bali - Kuala Lumpur – Cochin

    After breakfast, you will check out from hotel and will be driven to the airport for your departure flight to Cochin at 12.25 hrs. Travel back with wonderful memories of Fortune Bali tour!
