Lakshadweep Tour Packages

Lakshadweep, one of World’s most spectacular Tropical Island enclave of 36 exquisite coral islands is packed with exotic sun-kissed beaches, verdant coconut groves and lush green landscapes. The beautiful sandy beaches and gorgeous shallow lagoons embracing the atolls bequeaths you a ‘feeling of erratic utopia’. The underwater view at Lakshadweep is without doubt kaleidoscopic and breathtaking. Each island is fringed by snow white coral sands and against the vast expanse of the blue sea, the islets look like stunning emeralds. The lagoons also tenders excellent potential for water sports like swimming, wind-surfing, diving, snorkeling and kayaking. No wonder, Lakshadweep is fast becoming India’s one of its kind “Adventure sport – Nature Tourism” location. For an eager vacationers to Lakshadweep, we have designed unique and exclusive tour packages catering to your special requirements like Honeymoon Holidays, Adventure and Expedition Tours and Eco-tourism to name a few. We also organize tailor-made tour packages that can be fully customized according to your travel spots and plans. Cherry-pick one of the popular packages and we guarantee you an unforgettable holiday experience in your dream destination.
